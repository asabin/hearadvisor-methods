# Metrics

Earplug performance is evaluated via perceptual models that have been shown to predict human judgements of loudness and sound quality.

| Metric | What it Measures |
|---|---|
| [Perceptual Loudness Reduction](loudness-reduction.md) | How much quieter the environment sounds |
| [Perceptual Sound Quality](sound-quality.md) | How natural the sound remains |
| [SoundScore & SoundGrade](soundscore.md) | Weighted composite and letter grade |

!!! note
    We recognize that additional non-audio dimensions (e.g., comfort, style, durability) are important to consumers, but are not considered in these metrics.
